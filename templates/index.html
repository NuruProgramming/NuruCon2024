About Nuru and Nuru Con

What is Nuru?

Nuru is a groundbreaking Swahili programming language designed to make coding accessible to Swahili speakers. It bridges the gap between technology and language, empowering millions to learn and create in their native tongue.

What is Nuru Con?

Nuru Con is the first-ever meetup for Nuru enthusiasts, developers, and learners. It's a platform to come together, share knowledge, and explore the endless possibilities of this innovative language.
